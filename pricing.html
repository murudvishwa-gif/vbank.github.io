Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Are there any hidden charges?

No. Every fee is listed upfront in your plan — there are no maintenance charges, hidden transfer fees, or surprise deductions on any Stackly account.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from the app, with changes applied from your next billing cycle.

Is the Basic plan really free forever?

Yes — Basic has no monthly fee, no minimum balance requirement, and no expiry. It's a complete savings account at no cost.

Do business accounts charge per employee?

No. The Business plan includes payroll and multi-user access for unlimited employees at a single flat monthly rate.

What happens if I cancel Premium?

Your account automatically reverts to Basic with no loss of funds or account history — your savings, cards, and transaction history remain fully intact.
