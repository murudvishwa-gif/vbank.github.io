Our Story

Built to make banking feel human again

Stackly started with a simple frustration: banking was slow, confusing, and built for the bank's convenience — not yours. Twelve years later, we serve millions of customers who expect more from their money.

Our Mission

Make every financial decision simpler, faster, and fairer

We believe access to clear, honest banking shouldn't depend on your income, your location, or how much time you have to stand in a queue. Every product we build — from a first savings account to a multi-crore business loan — starts with the same question: does this make the customer's life easier?

That principle has shaped everything from our zero-fee savings accounts to our 48-hour loan approvals and our decision to keep human support available around the clock.

"Banking should fit your life, not interrupt it."

— Stackly Founding Principle, 2014

Our Journey

Twelve years of building toward better banking

01

2014 — Founded in Bengaluru

Stackly launched as a digital-only savings platform, aiming to remove the friction of traditional branch banking for India's first generation of smartphone-first customers.

02

2017 — RBI Banking License & Lending Launch

We became a fully licensed bank, introducing home loans, personal loans, and credit cards with transparent, fee-free terms.

03

2020 — Business Banking & Payroll

As small businesses went digital overnight, we launched Stackly Business — current accounts, invoicing, and payroll tools built for teams of any size.

04

2023 — NRI & Global Remittance

We expanded support for India's global diaspora with NRE/NRO accounts and same-day international transfers to over 40 countries.

05

2026 — 2.8 Million Customers and Growing

Today, Stackly manages over ₹4.2 trillion in assets across individuals and businesses, with a 99.9% platform uptime and 24/7 human support.
